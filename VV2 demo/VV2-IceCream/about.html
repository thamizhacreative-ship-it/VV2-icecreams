Who We Are

Made with Premium Cow Milk & Real Passion

VV2 Stone Cold Ice Creams was founded by M.V. Arun Kumar with one clear mission — to bring genuinely premium, 100% vegetarian ice cream to Chennai's families, weddings, and celebrations.

Operating out of Kilpauk, Chennai, we are proud official partners of Baskin-Robbins and Mercely's Ice Cream — two of the world's most respected ice cream brands. Every product we serve meets the quality standards of both brands while carrying our own signature touch.

All our ice creams are made exclusively with premium quality cow milk — no shortcuts, no compromises. From real fruit scoops to signature event desserts, every scoop is crafted to be an experience, not just a treat.

Our Journey

How VV2 Grew

The Beginning

Founded in Kilpauk, Chennai

M.V. Arun Kumar founded VV2 with a vision to offer premium vegetarian ice cream made from fresh cow milk — filling a gap in Chennai's market for quality event desserts.

Partnership

Official Mercely's Partner

VV2 became an authorised partner of Mercely's Ice Cream, bringing premium Mercely's stick variants and products to Chennai's events and celebrations.

Growth

Baskin-Robbins Partnership

A major milestone — VV2 became a Baskin-Robbins partner, bringing the world-famous BR Rock Ice Cream and premium sundae range to all events we serve.

Today

Chennai's Go-To Event Ice Cream

Thousands of events served across Chennai. New flavours including Lotus Biscoff, Triple Cheers Chocolate, and Very Berry Strawberry added to our growing menu.
